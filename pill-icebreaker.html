Icebreaker Activity

The Super
Pill Dilemma

You can only choose ONE. Which superpower is worth the price you'd pay?

🧠
Omniscience
The All-Knowing
⚡ The Power
You instantly know the answer to any question — history, science, secrets, stock prices, exam answers. You are the smartest person who has ever lived.
⚠️ The Catch
You can never stop knowing. You hear every thought of every person near you — their fears, lies, and feelings about you. You cannot turn it off. Ever.
⏳
Time Freeze
Master of Moments
⚡ The Power
Pause time at will. Use it to sleep, prepare, avoid danger, or live an entire lifetime in a single afternoon. You never miss a deadline again.
⚠️ The Catch
Every hour you spend in frozen time ages you 10 minutes. Heavy users will watch themselves wrinkle while the world stands still.
👻
Invisibility
The Unseen
⚡ The Power
Vanish completely — no cameras, no sensors, no detection. Walk into any room, board any flight, escape any situation. Total freedom.
⚠️ The Catch
Every time you go invisible, one person you love forgets one memory of you. Use it too much and your closest relationships dissolve into nothing.
🩺
Immortality
The Undying
⚡ The Power
You cannot die. Disease, injury, old age — none of it applies. You'll witness centuries of history, technology, and human evolution firsthand.
⚠️ The Catch
Everyone you love ages and dies on a normal timeline. You'll bury every friend, every partner, every child — and still wake up the next morning.
✈️
Flight
The Untethered
⚡ The Power
Fly anywhere on Earth, any speed, any altitude. No passport, no tickets, no traffic. Just you and the open sky — completely free.
⚠️ The Catch
You can never ride in any vehicle again. No cars, planes, trains, boats, or elevators. If you can't fly there, you walk. Goodbye road trips and family vacations.
💊 How to play: Pick your pill and own it. Then go around the room — share which you chose and why. Bonus: try to convince someone else they made the wrong choice.
